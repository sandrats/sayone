BookMyEvents Superstar

It’s simple, really! Being a loyalty program, the only way to become a Superstar is to complete 10 bookings in the past 365 days. Bookings are considered eligible for Superstar status, only if the amount paid for the transaction is ₹100/- or above, which is valid for movie & non-movie transactions only. Once all requirements are met, you will see the Superstar badge in your 'Profile'. The profile will update at the end of the tenth show that is booked. Please make sure you have updated the app to it's latest available version.
